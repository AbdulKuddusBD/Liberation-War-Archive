About the archive

The Bangladesh Liberation War began after the military crackdown of 25 March 1971 and continued until the surrender of Pakistani forces on 16 December 1971. This archive presents a structured way to study documents, testimonies and visual records connected to that history.

The site is designed for students, researchers and general visitors who want a clear, responsive and accessible archive experience.

About the Archive

The Liberation War Archive is a comprehensive online repository dedicated to preserving the history, memories, and important documents of Bangladesh's 1971 Liberation War.

Our Mission

To document, preserve, and share the stories, struggles, and triumphs of the Bengali nation's nine-month war that led to Bangladesh's independence on December 16, 1971.

What We Preserve
  • Historical documents and declarations
  • Interviews with freedom fighters and witnesses
  • Photographs from the Liberation War period
  • Personal stories and testimonies
  • Official records and correspondence
Participate

There are many ways to help preserve our history:

  • Contribute Materials: Share documents, photographs, or recordings with us.
  • Share Stories: Tell your family's stories from the war.
  • Volunteer: Help us organize, digitize, and preserve materials.
  • Spread the Word: Share the archive with others.
  • Support: Help expand our digital infrastructure.
